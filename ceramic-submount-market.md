<p><p>The ceramic submount market primarily includes two types: alumina and aluminum nitride (AIN). Alumina submounts are prized for their excellent electrical insulation, thermal conductivity, and mechanical strength, making them suitable for various electronic applications. In contrast, AIN submounts offer superior thermal conductivity and a higher melting point, making them ideal for high-performance devices, such as LEDs and power electronics. Both materials facilitate reliable heat dissipation and enhance the performance of electronic components in demanding environments.</p></p>
