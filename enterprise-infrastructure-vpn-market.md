<p><p>The Enterprise Infrastructure VPN market consists of three main types: On-Premises, Managed, and Hybrid VPNs. On-Premises VPNs require organizations to install and maintain their own network infrastructure, offering full control but demanding significant resources. Managed VPNs are hosted and maintained by third-party providers, allowing businesses to reduce operational complexity while ensuring secure connectivity. Hybrid VPNs combine elements of both, leveraging on-premises and cloud resources to provide flexibility and scalability, catering to diverse organizational needs and varying workloads.</p></p>
