<p><p>The corrugated board packaging market is segmented into several types based on construction. Single-faced corrugated board features a fluted layer bonded to a flat liner, offering basic protection. Single-wall corrugated board consists of one layer of fluting between two liners, providing moderate strength. Twin-wall corrugated board has two layers of fluting with three liners, enhancing durability. Triple-wall corrugated board comprises three fluted layers with four liners, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Other types may include specialty designs for unique packaging needs.</p></p>
