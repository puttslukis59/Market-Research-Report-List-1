<p><p>The wire-cut EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) market features two primary types: Wire-Cut Flushing Type EDM and Wire-Cut Submerge Type EDM. The Flushing Type utilizes a fluid to enhance debris removal, improving cutting efficiency and surface finish, making it suitable for intricate designs. In contrast, the Submerge Type operates submerged in a dielectric fluid, minimizing thermal effects and promoting better precision, which is ideal for delicate materials and complex geometries. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications in precision machining.</p></p>
