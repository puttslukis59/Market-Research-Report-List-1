<p><p>Vehicle fire suppression systems are crucial for protecting vehicles, particularly in commercial and industrial applications. The market is primarily segmented into two types: wet systems and dry systems. Wet systems utilize water-based extinguishing agents, effectively cooling and suppressing flames through continuous discharge. In contrast, dry systems employ chemical agents, such as foams or powders, to smother fires, preventing re-ignition. Each system has distinct advantages, depending on factors like vehicle type, usage, and specific fire risks encountered.</p></p>
