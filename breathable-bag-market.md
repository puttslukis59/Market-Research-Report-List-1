<p><strong>The Breathable Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Perforation</li><li>Macro Perforation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The breathable bag market is segmented into micro perforation and macro perforation types, primarily based on the size of the holes designed for gas exchange. Micro perforation bags feature tiny holes that allow minimal air circulation, ideal for items needing slight ventilation without moisture loss, such as fresh produce. In contrast, macro perforation bags possess larger openings to facilitate higher air flow, making them suitable for products that require significant breathability, like floral arrangements or certain baked goods.</p></p>
