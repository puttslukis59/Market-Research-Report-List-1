<p><strong>The Vehicle Superchargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Roots</li><li>Twin-Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle supercharger market includes three primary types: centrifugal, Roots, and twin-screw. Centrifugal superchargers use a rotating impeller to compress air, offering high efficiency and power gains at high RPMs, making them suitable for performance-oriented applications. Roots superchargers rely on positive displacement, delivering a consistent airflow at lower RPMs, ideal for delivering immediate power. Twin-screw superchargers combine the benefits of both, providing high efficiency and increased pressure with compact design, making them popular in both performance and OEM applications.</p></p>
