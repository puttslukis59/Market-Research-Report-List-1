<p><ul><li>Pulsed Laser Diode Bar</li><li>CW Laser Diode Bar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Power Laser Diode Bar Modules market is divided into two main types: Pulsed Laser Diode Bars and Continuous Wave (CW) Laser Diode Bars. Pulsed Laser Diode Bars operate by emitting laser light in short bursts, making them ideal for applications requiring high peak power, such as material processing and medical treatments. In contrast, CW Laser Diode Bars provide a continuous output of laser light, suitable for applications like pumping solid-state lasers and industrial materials processing, where consistent performance is essential.</p></p>
