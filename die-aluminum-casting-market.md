<p><strong>The Die Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-chamber Die Casting</li><li>Cold-chamber Die Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die aluminum casting market encompasses two primary types: hot-chamber and cold-chamber die casting. Hot-chamber die casting involves injecting molten aluminum into the mold using a chamber that remains molten, suitable for metals with low melting points, ensuring high production rates and intricate designs. Conversely, cold-chamber die casting requires the metal to be melted in a separate furnace and then poured into the chamber for injection, making it ideal for aluminum and high-precision applications, albeit at a slower production rate.</p></p>
