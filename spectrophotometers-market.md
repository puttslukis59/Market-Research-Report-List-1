<p><strong>The Spectrophotometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-Visible Spectrometry</li><li>IR- Spectrometry</li><li>Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR)</li><li>Near Infrared Spectrometry</li><li>Raman Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectrophotometer market comprises various types based on their working principles and applications. UV-Visible spectrometers analyze light absorption in the UV and visible spectrum, often used in chemical analysis. Infrared (IR) spectrometry measures molecular vibrations, analyzing organic compounds. Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) provides detailed molecular structures by observing nuclear spin properties. Near Infrared Spectrometry examines overtones and combination vibrations, useful in agriculture and food industries. Raman spectrometry utilizes inelastic light scattering for chemical composition analysis, widely applied in material science and biochemistry.</p></p>
