<p><p>The Solar Automatic Tracking System market consists of two main types: Single Axis and Dual Axis systems. Single Axis Solar Trackers rotate on one pivot point, maximizing solar capture throughout the day by following the sun's east-to-west movement. In contrast, Dual Axis Solar Trackers adjust both horizontally and vertically, allowing them to follow the sun's seasonal trajectory, thereby increasing energy efficiency. Both systems enhance the performance of solar installations, catering to different energy output requirements and site conditions.</p></p>
