<p><p>Capsule endoscopy diagnostics is a non-invasive imaging technique used primarily to visualize the gastrointestinal (GI) tract, particularly the small intestine, to identify conditions such as bleeding, tumors, or inflammatory diseases. The technology involves swallowing a small, pill-sized camera that captures images as it passes through the digestive system, which are transmitted wirelessly to a recorder worn by the patient.</p><p>The Capsule Endoscopy Diagnostics Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing prevalence of GI disorders, advancing technology, and growing patient preference for non-invasive procedures.
