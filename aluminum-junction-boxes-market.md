<p><strong>The Aluminum Junction Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IP66</li><li>IP54</li><li>IP67</li><li>IP55</li><li>IP65</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum junction boxes are essential in various industries, offering protection for electrical connections. They are categorized by their Ingress Protection (IP) ratings, which indicate their resistance to dust and water. IP66 provides complete dust ingress protection and high-pressure water resistance. IP54 offers limited dust ingress and water splashes protection. IP67 ensures submersion in water up to 1 meter, while IP55 protects against dust and low-pressure water jets. IP65 guarantees total dust protection and water jet resistance, making these boxes versatile in outdoor and industrial settings.</p></p>
