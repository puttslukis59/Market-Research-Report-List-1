<p><p>Corrugated metal roofing sheets are primarily categorized into three types: galvanized steel, galvalume steel, and others. Galvanized steel, coated with zinc, offers corrosion resistance and durability, making it a popular choice for various applications. Galvalume steel features a combination of zinc and aluminum, providing superior rust protection and a longer lifespan. Other materials may include aluminum and stainless steel, offering unique properties like lightweight, malleability, and enhanced aesthetics. Each type caters to different project requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
