<p><p>Third Party Logistics (3PL) software is categorized into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based 3PL software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with an internet connection. It typically involves subscription pricing and automatic updates. On-premise software, in contrast, is installed directly on a company's hardware, providing more control over data security and system customization but requiring significant upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to varying business needs and IT capabilities.</p></p>
