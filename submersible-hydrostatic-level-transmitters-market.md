<p><p>Submersible hydrostatic level transmitters are essential for measuring fluid levels in tanks and wells. The market is segmented into flanged and threaded types. Flanged transmitters feature a flat surface for bolting to a tank, providing a secure and leak-proof seal, ideal for larger installations and higher pressure applications. Threaded transmitters utilize a screw connection, offering ease of installation and flexibility for smaller or space-constrained environments. Both types ensure accurate level measurement but are suited to different operational needs and configurations.</p></p>
