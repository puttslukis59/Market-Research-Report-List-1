<p><p>Pipe jacking service is a trenchless construction technique used for the installation of pipelines beneath the ground without extensive surface disruption. This method involves the use of a jacking machine that pushes pipes through the soil, making it suitable for urban areas where open-cut excavation would be impractical. This service is essential for utility installations, such as water, sewage, and telecommunication lines, particularly in congested urban environments.</p><p>The Pipe Jacking Service Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period.
