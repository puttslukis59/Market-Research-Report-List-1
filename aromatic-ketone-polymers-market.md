<p><p>The aromatic ketone polymers market comprises three main types: PEEK, PEKK, and PEK. PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) is known for its high thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. PEKK (Polyetherketoneketone) offers enhanced chemical resistance and processing versatility, appealing to industries like oil and gas. PEK (Polyetherketone) features good thermal and chemical resistance, often used in electrical and electronic applications. Together, these polymers serve specialized sectors requiring durable, lightweight materials.</p></p>
