<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Specialty Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tires</li><li>Bias (Crossply) Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial specialty tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tires and bias (crossply) tires. Radial tires feature reinforced steel belts, providing better durability, heat dissipation, and improved fuel efficiency, making them ideal for long-distance transportation and heavy loads. Conversely, bias tires have a design with diagonal layers of fabric, offering a stiffer sidewall and greater load capacity, making them suitable for off-road and short-haul applications. Each type serves specific needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
