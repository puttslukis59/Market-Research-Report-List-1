<p><ul><li>Film Radiography</li><li>Computed Radiography</li><li>Direct Radiography</li><li>Computed Tomography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-ray inspection equipment and imaging software encompass various technologies for non-destructive testing. Film radiography utilizes traditional film methods to capture images, while computed radiography employs digital imaging plates for better flexibility and fewer chemical processes. Direct radiography offers real-time image capture using digital sensors, enhancing speed and efficiency. Computed tomography provides 3D imaging by taking multiple X-ray images from different angles, allowing for detailed internal assessment. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring quality and safety across industries.</p></p>
