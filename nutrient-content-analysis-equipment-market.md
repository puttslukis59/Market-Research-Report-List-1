<p><strong>The Nutrient Content Analysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nutrient content analysis equipment primarily utilizes DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology and NIR (Near-Infrared) technology for assessing the composition of food products. DEXA technology provides precise quantitative measurements of fat and lean tissue by analyzing X-ray absorption, making it ideal for nutritional studies. In contrast, NIR technology employs light absorption patterns to quickly estimate moisture, protein, and fat content in various materials, offering rapid, non-destructive analysis suitable for food quality control and research applications.</p></p>
