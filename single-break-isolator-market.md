<p><strong>The Single Break Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Break Isolator market is categorized into three main types: Bus Side, Line Side, and Transfer Bus Side. Bus Side isolators are positioned on the bus bar and manage power distribution among multiple circuits. Line Side isolators are installed between the power source and the load, ensuring safe disconnection for maintenance. Transfer Bus Side isolators facilitate switching between two power sources or transformers, allowing seamless transfer while maintaining system reliability and preventing downtime during maintenance.</p></p>
