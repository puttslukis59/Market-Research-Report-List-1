<p><p>Circumferential piston pumps utilize a distinct mechanism for fluid transfer, featuring two primary market types based on sealing configurations. Single mechanical seal pumps are simpler, providing effective sealing for many applications but may require more frequent maintenance in harsh environments. In contrast, double mechanical seal pumps offer enhanced reliability and leakage protection, making them ideal for critical applications or hazardous materials. The choice between these types depends on specific operational requirements, including fluid characteristics and environmental conditions.</p></p>
