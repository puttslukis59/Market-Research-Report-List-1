<p><strong>The Profilometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Profilometer</li><li>Non-contact Profilometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The profilometer market is segmented into contact and non-contact types. Contact profilometers utilize a probe that physically touches the surface to measure its texture and topography, providing high accuracy for various materials. In contrast, non-contact profilometers employ optical or laser techniques to assess surface profiles without physical contact, making them suitable for delicate or soft materials. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, aerospace, and semiconductor manufacturing, where precise surface measurement is critical for quality control and product performance.</p></p>
