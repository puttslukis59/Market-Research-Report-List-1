<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBC) consist of three main types: Styrene Butadiene Styrene (SBS), Styrene Isoprene Styrene (SIS), and Hydrogenated Styrenic Block Copolymers (HSBC). SBS offers excellent elasticity and is commonly used in adhesives and sealants. SIS provides enhanced flexibility and is ideal for applications like roofing membranes and medical devices. HSBC, being a modified version of SBS, combines strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Together, they serve diverse industries, enhancing performance and versatility.</p></p>
