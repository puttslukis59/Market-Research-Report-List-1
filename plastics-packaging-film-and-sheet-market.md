<p><p>The plastics packaging film and sheet market encompasses various materials, primarily including polyethylene (PE), biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP), and biaxially oriented polyethylene terephthalate (BOPET). PE films are versatile and widely used for their moisture barrier properties, making them ideal for food packaging. BOPP films are known for their clarity, strength, and high tensile properties, suitable for labels and flexible packaging. BOPET films offer excellent dimensional stability, barrier qualities, and are often used in high-performance applications, including electronics and food preservation.</p></p>
