<p><strong>The Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Constellations</li><li>Regional Constellations</li><li>Satellite-Based Augmentations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS market comprises various types of positioning systems. Global Constellations, like GPS and GLONASS, provide worldwide coverage, enabling accurate location services globally. Regional Constellations, such as Galileo and BeiDou, focus on specific areas, enhancing precision and availability in those regions. Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS) improve the accuracy and reliability of GNSS signals by providing correction data, benefiting applications like aviation and land surveying. Together, these systems enhance navigation, timing, and geolocation across diverse sectors.</p></p>
