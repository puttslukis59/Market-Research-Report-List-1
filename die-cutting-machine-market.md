<p><p>Die cutting machines are essential in various manufacturing processes, categorized into several types based on their applications. Metal-to-metal die cutting involves precise cutting of metal sheets for forming parts. Perfing creates perforated lines for easy tearing. Slitting separates materials into narrower widths. Kiss cutting cuts through the top layer of material while leaving the backing intact, ideal for stickers and labels. Scoring produces indentation lines for folding, enhancing packaging designs. Each type serves unique requirements across industries.</p></p>
