<p><p>Non-contact position sensors encompass technologies that detect position without physical contact, ensuring durability and reliability. Linear position sensors measure displacement along a straight path, often utilizing technologies like inductive or capacitive sensing for precise readings. Rotary position sensors, on the other hand, monitor angular positions and rotations, employing methods such as optical or magnetic sensing. Both types are crucial across various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation, enhancing functionality and performance in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
