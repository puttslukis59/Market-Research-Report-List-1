<p><strong>The Video Otoscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>S-Video Video Output</li><li>Composite Video Output</li><li>HDMI Video Output</li><li>DVI Video Output</li><li>USB Video Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Video Otoscope market offers various output types to meet different user needs. S-Video output provides better resolution than composite video, making it suitable for detailed examinations. Composite video output is a standard format, widely compatible but lower in quality. HDMI video output delivers high-definition video, enhancing image clarity for detailed diagnostics. DVI video output offers a digital signal with high quality, preferred in professional settings. USB video output enables easy connectivity and digital storage, appealing for modern applications.</p></p>
