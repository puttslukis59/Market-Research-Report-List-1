<p><ul><li>Half Hourly or Non-Half Hourly Meter</li><li>Automated Meter Reading (AMR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-Phase Meter market comprises three primary types: Half Hourly (HH) Metering, Non-Half Hourly (NHH) Metering, and Automated Meter Reading (AMR). Half Hourly Meters capture energy consumption data every 30 minutes, facilitating real-time analysis for businesses with high energy needs. Non-Half Hourly Meters record usage less frequently, suitable for smaller consumers. AMR technology automates data collection, ensuring accurate, timely readings that enhance operational efficiency and allow for remote monitoring, benefiting both suppliers and end-users.</p></p>
