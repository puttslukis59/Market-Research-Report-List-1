<p><p>The IR LED market is segmented into two main types: IR LED chips and IR LED packages. IR LED packages, on the other hand, consist of encased IR LED chips, offering protection and enhanced efficiency. These packages are commonly utilized in consumer electronics, automotive, and security systems, emphasizing the growing demand for IR technology across various sectors.</p></p>
