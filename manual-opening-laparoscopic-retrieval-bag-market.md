<p><p>The manual opening laparoscopic retrieval bag market is categorized into two main types: detachable and non-detachable retrieval bags. Detachable retrieval bags can be easily separated from the instrument once the surgical contents are secured, allowing for convenient removal. Conversely, non-detachable retrieval bags remain attached during use, providing continuous access and control. Both types are designed to facilitate the safe extraction of tissue and foreign materials during laparoscopic procedures, enhancing surgical efficiency and patient safety.</p></p>
